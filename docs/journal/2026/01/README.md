## Important Notes

- Files in this directory are **NOT canonical specifications or design documents**.
- Contents may be revised, summarized, reorganized, or partially removed over time.
- Deletion should be **intentional and reviewed**, not accidental.

Canonical documents live elsewhere:

- **Specifications**: `docs/spec/`
- **Design records**: `docs/design/`
- **Phase summaries**: `docs/notes/`

This journal must not be treated as an authoritative source.

---

## Relationship to Phases

Entries in this journal may reference:
- specific phases (e.g. Phase 2.8),
- work items (e.g. Work A1),
- or temporary decisions that later appear in official phase documents.

When a decision or design is finalized:
- it should be promoted to `docs/design/` or `docs/notes/`,
- and this journal should be updated to reference that promotion.

---

## Pruning and Summarization Policy

This journal is **kept by default**.

However, when it becomes too large or noisy:
- early entries may be **summarized and consolidated**,
- detailed drafts may be replaced with short summary documents
  (e.g. `2026-01-early-summary.md`).

The goal is to preserve **intent and reasoning**, not raw verbosity.
